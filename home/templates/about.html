About Odyssey-Studio

After graduating from university, I founded Odyssey-Studio. The name is deeply inspired by the journey of Odysseus in the battle of Troy, symbolizing the perseverance, resilience, and determination needed to overcome life's challenges. My journey mirrors this in many ways— despite facing the obstacle of being deaf, I am not deterred from pursuing a career as a web developer.

While many have denied me opportunities due to my disability, I have refused to let it stop me. My deafness does not define my abilities or my work ethic. I believe that every challenge is an opportunity for growth, and this mindset has driven me to gain invaluable skills and experience in web development. I have continued to build my knowledge and expertise in various technologies, and I am confident in my ability to contribute meaningfully to any project.

The name Odyssey-Studio reflects this journey—just like Odysseus, who faced adversity yet never gave up, I too have embarked on a path that demands resilience and perseverance. Odyssey-Studio represents not just my company but also my personal mission to break barriers and prove that disability does not limit potential.

You can find my CV showcasing the skills, experience, and determination I have gained along this path.
